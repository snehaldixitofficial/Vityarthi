Part B - Prevention Checklist
1. Basic Online Safety
* Always check the sender before clicking any link.
* Never share your OTPs, passwords, and personal information.
* Make strong passwords, and use different passwords for different accounts.
* Switch on two-factor authentication on apps and sites.
* Skip any shady or unknown links.
2. UPI and Money Tips
* Ignore payment requests from strangers.
* Always verify the UPI ID and who you're sending to before hitting send.
3. How to Report
* Go to https://cybercrime.gov.in to file a report.
* For money scams, call 1930 helpline right away.
